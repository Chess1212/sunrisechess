The Queen

A Sunrise Chess Production






The queen is one of the chess pieces. It can move in every direction. The queen like all other chess pieces can not move on a sq-

uare occupied by a piece of its own color. The queen is the most powerful piece because it has such a vast range, so players val-

ue it. Players do not normally trade their queen for other pieces. The queen starts on the first or eighth of the d file, on the

square next to the king. Players get only one queen at the start of the game, but can get others if they get to the other side of

the board, and I have covered that in the video about pawns.
