The King

A Sunrise Chess Production






The king is one of the chess pieces. It can move one square in one direction. The king like all other chess pieces can not move on

to a square occupied by a piece of its own color. Although weak, the king is the most important pieces because if it is attacked

and can not move anywhere without being attacked, then the king that can't move lost the game. The king starts on the first or e-

ighth of the e file, on the square opposite of it's own color. If the king is attacked by a piece of the opposite color, then the

king is in check. The king can't move into check. If the king is in check and the side can not move the king, block the check, or

capture the checking piece, then the king is in checkmate, losing the game.
